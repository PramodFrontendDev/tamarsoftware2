About Tamar Software

Empowering business growth with intelligent software engineering, reliable integrations, and transparent execution.

Tamar Software was founded in 2018 and has rapidly emerged to become a trusted leader in the IT industry by delivering cutting-edge software solutions. Addressing the evolving technology requirements of businesses across the world has made this software development company a leading choice for enterprises seeking reliable innovation and digital transformation.

In 2024, Tamar Software transitioned to a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) to support the rapid growth and scale of the organization. Today, we are fully equipped to empower business innovation and deliver superior IT and software development services aligned with our clients' strategic business goals.

At the helm of this dynamic organization is Pradeep Tamar Kumar, who brings decades of rich leadership experience in banking, insurance, and the IT sectors. His visionary leadership, rooted in integrity and passion, has been the driving force behind the growth and expansion of Tamar Software globally.

Our History

The Tamar Software Journey

A vertical timeline flow showcasing the milestones that define our growth and dedication.

2018

Foundation & Kickoff

Tamar Software was established in Noida, India, focusing on delivering custom web development and database engineering solutions for startups and local enterprises.

2020

Global Client Expansion

Expanded our remote client delivery framework to serve businesses across North America, Europe, and the Middle East, primarily in the banking and insurance domains.

2022

AI & ML Division Launch

Inaugurated our dedicated Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning department to provide advanced chatbot integrations, predictive dashboading, and data analytics.

2024

LLP Transition & Strategic Rebranding

Reorganized the corporate structure into a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) to scale engineering teams, enhance enterprise partnerships, and strengthen our service portfolio.

2026

Leading IT Transformation Partner

Now delivering 200+ successful projects globally with a team of 50+ senior engineers, setting state-of-the-art standards in mobile, cloud, and digital solutions.
